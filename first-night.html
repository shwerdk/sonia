Detailed Steps:
1. Gather Wood: Immediately after spawning, locate and break a few trees to obtain wood planks. This is crucial for crafting your first tools and shelter.
2. Craft Tools: Use the crafting table to create a basic wooden pickaxe, axe, and sword. These tools will be essential for further resource gathering and defense.
3. Build a Quick Shelter: Construct a basic shelter by walling yourself in with blocks like dirt or cobblestone. This provides a safe space during the night when hostile mobs are active.
4. Light Up Your Shelter: Place torches around your shelter to prevent hostile mobs from spawning nearby. Torches also provide light, making it easier to navigate your shelter.
5. Gather Food: Find and gather food sources like raw meat from passive mobs (cows, sheep, pigs) to prevent starvation. You can use your crafted tools to hunt these animals.
6. Set a Spawn Point: Craft a bed using wool from sheep and place it in your shelter to set a spawn point. This allows you to respawn safely if you die, and you can use it to skip the night.
7. Avoid Hostile Mobs: Stay in your shelter and avoid wandering around in the dark during the night to prevent encounters with hostile mobs like zombies, skeletons, and creepers.
8. Mine Stone: Once you have a wooden pickaxe, mine stone blocks to obtain cobblestone. Cobblestone is a more durable material for building, and it can be used to make better tools.
9. Craft a Furnace: Craft a furnace to cook raw meat, which provides better nutrition and health.
10. Prepare for the Next Day: Make sure you have enough food, wood, and tools to continue your exploration and construction during the next day.
